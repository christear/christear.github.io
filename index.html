Welcome

Hi! I have been working in the field of computational biology for over a decade, and have studied and lived in diverse cultural environments across different countries. I am currently an Assistant Professor at MBZUAI, where I am continuously looking for self-motivated students and postdocs to join my group. MBZUAI offers highly competitive, tax-free compensation and an excellent research environment.

My research mainly focus on AI for systems biology, leveraging deep learning–based AI technologies to model complex biological processes, such as gene regulation and antigen presentation. It aims to facilitate the discovery of novel biological phenomena, putative drug targets, innovative treatment strategies, and improved disease diagnostics.

I am actively looking for highly motivated PhD students, Master’s students, and interns. All stipends are tax-free.

If you are interested in AI for Science, particulally biological and biomedical science, please feel free to contact me at bin.zhang@mbzuai.ac.ae. Please prepare your email with:

  • Subject line: “RA/Master/PhD Prospective Student $YourName”
  • Highlight your publications (if applicable), GPA/ranking, TOEFL and IELTS scores
  • Attach your CV
